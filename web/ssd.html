⚡ The Honest Summary

This isn't a perfect dataset. No school schedule dataset is — districts don't publish in a standard format, and thousands of small districts barely have a web presence. Here's what we can promise:

  • Big districts are solid. Florida, Texas, Georgia, North Carolina — the major states have 77-100% confirmation rates. If your use case is weighted by student volume, you're in great shape.
  • Every row has data. Even unconfirmed districts have reasonable state-median estimates. You won't hit nulls.
  • Confidence is labeled. Every row has a confidence field — "confirmed" or "medium". Filter by confidence to suit your risk tolerance.
  • Small districts are the gap. Districts under 2,000 students make up most of the unconfirmed rows. If you're modeling national-scale patterns, the impact is minimal.
  • This is 2025-2026 only. We don't yet have multi-year historical data. That's planned for future releases.

API Access
