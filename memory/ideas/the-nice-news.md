# The Nice News
*Idea captured: 2026-01-28*

## Concept
A news bot / content brand that surfaces **inspiring, universally-loved stories** from the daily news cycle.

## How It Works
1. **Scan** daily news articles from across the internet
2. **Sentiment analysis** on responses/engagement to stories
3. **Filter** for high engagement + high positivity
4. **Curate** stories of:
   - Heroes
   - People coming together
   - Overcoming obstacles
   - Feel-good moments

## Distribution
- **X (Twitter)** — Share stories as they're found
- **Daily blog** — Summary of the best news
- **Newsletter** — Daily or weekly digest

## Why It Works
- News is overwhelmingly negative; people crave good news
- Sentiment analysis creates objective curation (not just vibes)
- "Universally loved" = cross-partisan appeal
- Shareable content = organic growth

## Open Questions

- What sentiment analysis tools/APIs?
- Automated or human-in-the-loop curation?
- Monetization? (Ads, sponsors, premium newsletter?)
- Brand voice — warm? journalistic? playful?
