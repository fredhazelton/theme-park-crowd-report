🔬 Idea Cycle × Team Integration

How each phase flows through the org structure with QA/QC loops
1 · Discover
Spot the Gap → Screen & Prioritize
👑 Fred + 🦴 Wilma + 🪨 Barney
Fred surfaces ideas from industry knowledge · Wilma scans data/trends · Barney researches feasibility · Ideas ranked by strategic fit
Fred ↔ Wilma Loop
Back-and-forth to refine ideas, kill weak ones early
▼
2 · Validate
Research & Design → Prototype
🦴 Wilma delegates → 🏏 Bam-Bam + 🪨 Barney
Bam-Bam builds MVP/prototype · Barney independently verifies approach & data sources · Pebbles designs mockups if needed
Barney ↔ Bam-Bam Loop
Code review + independent verification (the Accord pattern)
▼
3 · Build & Test
Run Pilot → Evaluate → Ship or Kill
🏏 Bam-Bam builds · 👽 Gazoo audits · 🦴 Wilma evaluates
Bam-Bam deploys pilot · Gazoo runs independent QA audit · Wilma compiles results · Fred makes go/no-go call
Gazoo ↔ Builder Loop
Independent audit — Gazoo catches what the builder missed
▼
4 · Launch
Go to Market → Monitor & Feedback
✍️ Betty + 🎀 Pebbles + 🦕 Dino
Betty writes launch content · Pebbles creates assets · Dino tracks tasks & metrics · Wilma monitors post-launch KPIs
Betty ↔ Pebbles Loop
Content + design QA — copy matches visuals, brand consistency
▼
5 · Grow
Evolve → back to Discover
🦴 Wilma + 👑 Fred
Wilma compiles user feedback & metrics · Fred reviews learnings · Together decide: iterate, expand, or start new cycle
Fred ↔ Wilma Loop
Strategic review — lessons learned feed next cycle
↩ Continuous loop — learnings feed back into Discover ↩
