## Product Vision

Theme Park Crowd Report is a **free, community-oriented** service that helps people plan better theme park visits using ML-powered crowd predictions and stunning visual data. The product grows organically through word-of-mouth, driven by genuinely helpful content that people want to share.

**Core principles:**

- **Free forever during beta.** No paywall, no catch. When premium tiers arrive, the free tier remains generous.
- **Community, not broadcast.** Members are participants, not subscribers. Their feedback directly shapes what we build.
- **Ever-improving.** Every interaction teaches us something. Usage patterns and feedback drive the roadmap.
- **Stunning visuals.** Our charts, heatmaps, and crowd reports should be beautiful enough that people screenshot and share them. Visual quality is a growth engine.
- **Organic growth.** No paid acquisition. The product is the marketing. If someone shares a crowd report or a bot answer, that's the best ad we could ever run.
