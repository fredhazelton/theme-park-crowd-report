The Key Insight

Tier 4 does the work. Tier 3 checks the work.

Expensive AI brains (Claude API) are reserved for judgment, not grunt work.
A local LLM handles code generation, data processing, drafting, and analysis at near-zero cost.
Specialist agents only activate to verify quality — review is cheaper than creation.

Result: Same quality output at a fraction of the cost

Phase 1 · Discover

  • 🔍Step 1 — Spot the Gap: Market intel, trend analysis, competitor scanning. Arnold (T4) runs automated scans. Barney verifies quality.
  • 🎯Step 2 — Screen & Prioritize: Rank ideas by impact × feasibility × strategic fit. Kill weak ideas before spending resources.
  • 👥Team: Fred + Wilma brainstorm · Arnold scans (T4 role) · Barney verifies intel
  • 🔄QA Loop: Fred ↔ Wilma strategic back-and-forth. Always active — every idea gets reviewed.

Phase 2 · Validate

  • 🧪Step 3 — Research & Design: Deep feasibility study, solution architecture, data source identification. T4 does the heavy lifting.
  • 🛠️Step 4 — Prototype: Build MVP / proof-of-concept. T4 writes the code. Senior agent verifies approach.
  • 👥Team: T4 builds · Bam-Bam QAs code · Barney QAs strategy
  • 🔄QA Loop: T3 ↔ T4 exchange, max 3 rounds. Adaptive sampling based on failure rate.

Phase 3 · Build & Test

  • 🚀Step 5 — Run Pilot: Deploy to limited audience, collect real-world data. T4 executes deployment.
  • 📊Step 6 — Evaluate: Measure KPIs against hypothesis. Assess product-market fit.
  • ⚖️Step 7 — Ship or Kill: The gate. Go to market, pivot, or dump. Gazoo has veto power.
  • 👥Team: T4 deploys · Gazoo audits independently · Wilma compiles · Fred decides
  • 🔄QA Loop: Always 100% review. Shipping decisions are never sampled.

Phase 4 · Launch + Phase 5 · Grow

  • 📢Step 8 — Go to Market: Launch strategy, positioning, pricing. T4 drafts content & assets.
  • 📡Step 9 — Monitor & Feedback: Track KPIs, gather user feedback. Dino tracks metrics.
  • 🔄Step 10 — Evolve: Iterate on feedback, optimize, expand. Then loop back to Step 1.
  • 👥Launch team: Betty QAs content · Pebbles QAs design · Cross-verified
  • 👥Grow team: Wilma aggregates everything · Fred makes strategic call on next cycle
