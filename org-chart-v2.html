⚡ Delegation & Work Model

Tier 4 does the work · Tier 3 verifies the work · Cost-optimized

How Work Flows

① Wilma Delegates
Wilma breaks tasks into units and sends them directly to Tier 4 (local LLM) for execution. Tier 4 does the heavy lifting — code, data processing, drafts, analysis — at near-zero cost.
② Tier 3 QA Reviews
A senior Tier 3 agent (paired by domain) reviews Tier 4's output. They verify quality, catch errors, and either approve, request revision (back to Tier 4), or escalate (to Wilma).
③ Adaptive Sampling
Not every task gets full QA. System uses random sampling based on historical failure rate. Low failures → spot checks. Rising failures → increased QA. Threshold breach → 100% review.

📊 Adaptive QA Rating System

Quality-driven sampling that scales review intensity with failure rates

QA Levels by Failure Rate

🟢 Green — Trusted
Failure rate: <5%
QA sampling: 10-20% of tasks
Random spot-checks only. Tier 4 output ships with light verification. Fastest, cheapest mode.
🟡 Yellow — Elevated
Failure rate: 5-15%
QA sampling: 50% of tasks
Something's off. Half the work gets reviewed. Track what's causing failures to find systemic issues.
🔴 Red — Full Review
Failure rate: >15%
QA sampling: 100% of tasks
Every output gets verified. Consider switching models, adjusting prompts, or escalating to Wilma for the entire workstream.
